Montego Bay
  • Pictures of Montego Bay

Montego Bay is a city that sits in the parish os Saint James on the island of Jamacia.

Montego Bay, also known as Mo Bay, is the city known for all of the awesome tourist attractions like Negril, Sandals Beach Resort, and contains some of the best places to catch an amazing view of the island.

For locals, many know Montego Bay as a great area to obtain a well paying job! The travel industry in Jamacia bring the island tons of revenue but Mo Bay is on of the largest contributors to that revenue surge.
